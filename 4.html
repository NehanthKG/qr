Electrical and Pneumatic Components in the Station

This station is equipped with a combination of electrical and pneumatic devices, each serving specific functions:

Electrical Components: These include Ultrasonic Sensors, Color Sensors, Capacitive Sensors, Thru Beam Sensors, DC Motors, Indicator Lamps, Push Buttons, and Toggle Switches.

Pneumatic Components: The pneumatic components consist of FRL (Filter-Regulator-Lubricator), Solenoid Valves, and Pneumatic Cylinders.

The primary objective of this station is to sort workpieces based on criteria such as orientation, depth, material type, and color.
