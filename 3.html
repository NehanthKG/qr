Human Machine Interface (HMI)

The Human Machine Interface (HMI) serves as the vital link between industrial processes and operators, essentially functioning as an operator's dashboard. It is the primary tool used by operators and line supervisors to coordinate and oversee manufacturing and industrial operations in a plant.

HMIs play a crucial role in simplifying complex process variables, making them understandable and actionable. They excel in displaying near real-time operational data, providing essential insights into the process. Visual process graphics offer clarity on the status of motors, valves, tank levels, and other critical parameters, enhancing control and optimization of production and process targets.
